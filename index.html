    Market

    The NuvoMed Blood Pressure Monitor can be brought at multiple locations. Places like CVS, Walmart, and Amazon. It is usually priced at around $60-$70. The one in my possession was on sale at CVS for $13.99. The NuvoMed can read a person’s systolic and diastolic blood pressure, while also reading the person’s heartbeat. Also, NuvoMed saves the last 20 readings, allowing the user to track their blood pressure history over time. When the NuvoMed is not on sale it can be quite expensive, but it is at the same level as the equipment that doctors usually use.
